Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your Carbon IQ account and all associated data at any time. This action is irreversible.

⚠️ What Will Be Deleted

  • Your vendor profile and registration details
  • All carbon emission records and scores
  • All form submissions and input data
  • All generated reports and history

📱 How to Delete From the App

1
Open the Carbon IQ app on your device
2
On the Home screen, scroll down to the Account section at the bottom
3
Tap Delete Account
4
Enter your Vendor ID and type DELETE to confirm
5
Tap Delete My Account Permanently — all your data will be immediately removed

⚠️ This cannot be undone. Once deleted, your Vendor ID, emission history, and all reports are permanently erased from our servers and cannot be recovered.
